SPIDER

Today i am going to talk about the characteristics of the spiders:

Spider Characteristics

  1. They have eight legs.
    • Use them for both walking and capturing prey.
  2. The body is divided into two parts: the cephalothorax and the abdomen.
    • Cephalothorax: which houses the head and legs and Abdomen: which contains the vital organs.
  3. Most spiders are carnivorous and hunters.
    • Actively seeking and capturing their prey.
  4. They produce silk that they use to build webs or capture prey.
  5. They have chelicerae (pincher-like appendages) used to inject venom into their prey.
    • With different types of silk serving various functions in their lives.
  6. They breathe through small organs called spiracles.
    • Which allow them to exchange gases with their environment.
  7. Most spiders are venomous, although the severity of the venom varies by species.
    • With some having more potent venom than others.
  8. They are typically solitary and avoid contact with other spiders unless it's mating time.
    • Minimizing competition for resources.
